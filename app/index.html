Almost all aspects of life the universe and everything can be mapped out using a network of nodes (concepts) and links (relationships). This page is built on that idea using a force directed graph, jQuery plug-in Library, that I built in 2010. All nodes in all graphs I have had the pleasure of using are the same size, but I found the true graphical representation of nodes not to be so. My goal was to build a force directed graph that would work with nodes of varying sizes.

  • You can move all nodes by clicking or touching the page and dragging.
  • You may also click or touch individual nodes and drag them to lock to specific locations.
  • Mouse Wheel zooms in and out, also up / down arrow keys and of course pinch.
  • Double clicking or taping resets zoom and will also recenter graph.
